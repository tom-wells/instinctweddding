Q.

Who will capture my wedding?

A.

Whether you book photography or video package, you will always have Justina or Lloyd with you and in most cases another one or two of our team members working with them.

Q.

Will we meet before my wedding?

A.

We love to meet our couples before the big day, either in real life or via video chat. More and more couples choose to have engagement, or pre-wedding sessions and its a great opportunity for us to meet and get to know each other before the big day.

Q.

I’m shy in front of the camera, will I have to pose a lot?

A.

We love capturing the real moments and emotions as they happen. For majority of the day we love to stay invisible and capture your day as it is, just from the most beautiful angle so you wouldn’t even notice us there. During the photo hour, we will give your gentle guidance, making sure you feel relaxed and look your best, but we never ask you to do awkward and unnatural cliche poses.

Q.

Do you offer wedding albums?

A.

Yes. You can order your wedding album once you receive your photo gallery. The process is easy and quick and we will be happy to guide you along the way.

Q.

Who do I contact if I have any questions or concerns before my wedding?

A.

Justina is responsible for all client communication, planning and making sure your day is perfectly captured. You can always email, text or call her and she always get back to you within 24 hours.

Q.

How to book Instinct Wedding to capture our wedding?

A.

Simply click our contact button and send us a message. Tell us a little bit about you and your wedding plans, including date and venue. We will get back to you ASAP (always within 24 hours).

Q.

Do you use contracts?

A.

Yes! We use a very easy to use online contracts, which means you can read, sign and return your contract in minutes without a need to print anything out.

Q.

Do you have insurance?

A.

Yes we have all relevant insurance, including public liability insurance.

Q.

Do you charge for travel?

A.

We cover weddings all over UK and Europe. If you like us to shoot your wedding more than 50miles away from Tunbridge Wells (that’s where we are based), we charge 45p per mile after that and ask client to pay for accommodation if needed too.

Q.

Do you offer payment plans?

A.

We ask for 20% deposit at the time of signing the contract and 80% is due two weeks before the wedding.

Q.

How soon after the wedding will I receive my photos and videos?

A.

Our current editing time is 15 weeks, however there is an option for a speedy edit if that’s what you need - let us know!
